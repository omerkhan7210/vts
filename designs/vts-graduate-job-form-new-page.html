Your Privacy — You Control What's Shared

Your submission does not go live automatically. VTS staff reviews every entry before it appears on the board. You control exactly what information is displayed publicly — check only what you're comfortable sharing.

I give VTS permission to add my job landing to the Job Landings Board Required
VTS will review and approve before posting. Without this consent, your submission is recorded for internal tracking only.
VTS may display my full name on the board
If unchecked, only your first name and last initial will be shown (e.g. "James R.").
VTS may include my starting salary in public board statistics
Used for the "Average Starting Salary" counter shown on the board. Your individual salary is never displayed — only the aggregate average.
VTS may display my success note / story on the board
Your written response to "How did VTS help you?" will appear as a quote with your entry.
VTS may use my photo alongside my board entry
If checked, a VTS staff member may follow up to request a photo. No photo will be used without your final approval.
📋
Every submission is reviewed by VTS staff before appearing on the board. This typically takes 1–3 business days. You may receive a brief follow-up if any information needs clarification.

Your data stays inside the VTS WordPress system. It is never sold or shared with third parties.
