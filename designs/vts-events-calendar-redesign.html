Free Veteran Training in San Diego

Veterans Transition Support has been providing free career certifications and training to veterans, transitioning service members, and military spouses in San Diego County since 2014. Classes are held at Camp Pendleton, in San Marcos, and online via Zoom — so you can attend from anywhere.

What Certifications Do We Offer?

  • OSHA 30-Hour Construction Safety
  • OSHA 10-Hour Construction Safety
  • Lean Six Sigma Yellow Belt (LSSYB)
  • Lean Six Sigma Green Belt (LSSGB)
  • Free Coursera Career Certificates
  • VA Disability & Benefits Workshops
  • Career Development & Resume Classes

Who Can Attend?

All VTS events are open at no cost to:

  • Veterans (all branches)
  • Transitioning active duty service members
  • Military spouses
  • Reservists and National Guard members
  • Wounded Warriors at Camp Pendleton
