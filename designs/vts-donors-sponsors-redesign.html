About VTS

Veterans Transition Support is an all-volunteer 501(c)(3) nonprofit based in San Marcos, CA. Founded in 2014, VTS provides free career certifications, training, and counseling to veterans and military families across San Diego, Orange County, and online — preventing unemployment, homelessness, and family disintegration through workforce readiness.

Where Your Money Goes

VTS has no paid staff. Every donated dollar funds program delivery — OSHA certification fees, Lean Six Sigma training materials, Coursera access, Zoom licensing, and classroom costs at Camp Pendleton and in San Marcos. We are transparent about our finances and share our IRS Form 990 upon request.

Corporate Sponsorship Benefits

VTS sponsors connect with a highly engaged military-connected community in San Diego County — veterans, service members, spouses, and their families and employers. Sponsorship demonstrates your organization's commitment to veteran employment and community service, qualifying for CSR recognition and community impact reporting.
