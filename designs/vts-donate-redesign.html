About VTS

Veterans Transition Support (VTS) is an all-volunteer 501(c)(3) nonprofit founded in 2014 and based in San Marcos, California. We provide free OSHA certifications, Lean Six Sigma training, Coursera access, and career counseling to veterans, transitioning service members, reservists, and military spouses across San Diego and Orange County.

Tax Deductibility

VTS is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. All donations are tax-deductible to the extent permitted by law. Upon donation you will receive an automatic email receipt for your records. For questions about your donation receipt or our EIN, contact us directly.

Donate to Veterans in San Diego

Every contribution — from a $25 one-time donation to a $1,000 cohort sponsorship — funds programs that help veterans earn industry-recognized certifications and launch careers after military service. VTS has trained 500+ veterans since 2014 at no cost to participants.
