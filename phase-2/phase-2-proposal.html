Phase 3 — Strategic Suggestion Only

An In-House LMS — Reducing Coursera Dependency

VTS routes all training through Coursera — a platform you don't control. If Coursera changes its pricing, access terms, or partner requirements, VTS has no fallback. A lightweight LMS built inside WordPress (LearnDash or Tutor LMS) would let VTS host supplemental content — orientation modules, resume workshops, soft-skills tracks — and over time, build toward a fully self-contained training platform on VTS's own domain.

  • ✦ Host VTS-owned course content — no third-party platform dependency
  • ✦ Issue VTS-branded certificates — your records, your recognition
  • ✦ Track learner progress and completion in-house, integrated with FluentCRM
  • ✦ Complement Coursera first, reduce dependency gradually — not an overnight switch
  • ✦ Volunteer & staff onboarding — internal training managed and tracked from day one
