About VTS

We exist for one reason — veterans succeed

Veterans Transition Support is a 100% all-volunteer nonprofit based in San Diego County. Founded in 2014 by Russell Levy J.D., VTS bridges the gap between military service and civilian career success — through free training, mentorship, and real-world certification programs that employers recognize and value.
